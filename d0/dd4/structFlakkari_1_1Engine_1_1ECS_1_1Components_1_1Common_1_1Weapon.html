Detailed Description

Weapon is a structure that defines the characteristics of a weapon.

The Weapon structure is used to define the characteristics of a weapon. It is used by the WeaponComponent to handle the firing and reloading logic.

Parameters
damageThe amount of damage dealt by the weapon. fireRate: The rate of fire, shots per second. currentAmmo: Current ammunition in the magazine. reloadTime: Time it takes to reload the weapon in seconds. timeSinceLastShot: Time elapsed since the last shot was fired. isReloading: Is the weapon currently reloading. maxAmmo: Maximum ammunition the weapon can hold.
